Youtube to Spotify Playlist Converter

created with HTML/Javascript

Unlike most people, I actually use YouTube to listen to music on my computer. The main reason is the same as why I still have a Yahoo mailbox: it's just too much work to switch services. Having a Chrome tab open with a Youtube video playing can be demanding of my laptop while I am working, so I decided to make this playlist converter. It could still use more features such as allowing YouTube users to log in or allowing the user to make sure every track added is correct, but I have put it on hold for now.
